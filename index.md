# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 January 2026

## 1. Introduction
This privacy policy explains how user data is collected, used, and protected when interacting with our AI-powered assistant via WhatsApp.

## 2. Data Collected
- WhatsApp phone number  
- Messages exchanged with the AI assistant  
- Information voluntarily provided by the user  
- Interaction metadata (timestamps, message type)

## 3. Use of Data
Data is used only to:
- Provide and operate the AI assistant
- Execute user-requested automated tasks
- Improve service reliability and security

## 4. Data Sharing
Data is not sold or shared with third parties, except when required by law or for essential technical services (hosting, AI APIs).

This service operates in compliance with Meta Platforms, Inc. policies.

## 5. Data Retention
Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service or to meet legal obligations.

## 6. Security
Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect user data.

## 7. User Rights
Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data.

## 8. Data Deletion
Users can request data deletion by sending "DELETE MY DATA" via WhatsApp or by email.
